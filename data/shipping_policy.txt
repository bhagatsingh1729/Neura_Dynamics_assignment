SHIPPING POLICY

1. Order Processing Time
All orders are processed within one (1) to two (2) business days, excluding weekends and
public holidays. Orders placed after business hours will be processed on the next
business day.

2. Shipping Destinations
Currently, we ship only to domestic addresses within the country. International shipping
is not available at this time.

3. Shipping Methods
We offer standard shipping for all orders. Expedited or express shipping options may be
introduced in the future but are not currently supported.

4. Delivery Time Estimates
Standard domestic shipping typically takes five (5) to seven (7) business days from the
date of shipment. Delivery times may vary depending on location, weather conditions, and
carrier delays.

5. Shipping Charges
Shipping charges are calculated at checkout and vary based on order value and delivery
location. Any applicable taxes or duties are the responsibility of the customer.

7. Delayed or Lost Shipments
If a shipment is delayed beyond the estimated delivery time, customers are encouraged to
contact customer support for assistance. In cases where a shipment is confirmed lost, a
replacement or refund may be issued after investigation.

8. Incorrect Shipping Information
Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping information. We are not liable
for delays or delivery failures resulting from incorrect or incomplete address details.

9. Policy Revisions
This Shipping Policy may be updated periodically. Customers should review this policy
regularly to stay informed of any changes.
